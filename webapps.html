Lingle

Oven

With the Oven, you can convert images to 1-bit! While specifically made to create assets for development on the Playdate handheld console, you can also convert images for just about anything else. It supports various types of ordered dithering (including blue noise), and even lets you modify the way the image gets converted to monochromatic.

Go to web app

Counter

The Counter lets you tally anything, saving it to your browser’s local storage. You can add multiple counters, change their names and colours, and all of it will still be there even if you refresh the page.
